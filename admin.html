Quick Guide

Upload Game:
  1. Isi data game (title, slug, description)
  2. Pilih story sections
  3. Drag & drop thumbnail image
  4. Klik "Create Game"
  5. Sistem akan otomatis upload gambar
Add Chapter:
  1. Pilih game dari dropdown
  2. Pilih section (Main Story, Character Story, dll)
  3. Isi judul chapter
  4. Tulis konten chapter
  5. Klik "Add Chapter"
Secure: Semua upload dilakukan melalui sistem yang aman.
